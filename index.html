How It Works

1

Sign Up

Create your account as a vehicle owner or rider. Quick verification process ensures safety for everyone.

2

Post or Search

Vehicle owners post trips with departure, arrival cities/areas, date, time, and available seats. Riders browse and search.

3

Book & Travel

Riders book seats or send requests. Connect through secure messaging and travel together safely.
